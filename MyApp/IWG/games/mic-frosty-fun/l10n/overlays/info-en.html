Game Rules

How to Play

  • Select your ticket cost.
  • Press PLAY or the spacebar to purchase and automatically reveal your ticket.
  • Blocks will fall onto the board. Reveal 3 or more matching blocks that share a side to automatically remove that cluster of blocks from the board and win according to the prize table.
  • For each block removed, a block falls down from above to fill its place, potentially forming more winning clusters.
  • If 5 winning clusters are removed in a single play, multiply your winnings by 2x!
  • Reveal 3 BONUS blocks to unlock the Wheel Bonus.
  • Reveal 3 GIFT blocks to unlock the Gift Bonus.
  • Reveal 3 FREE PLAY blocks to immediately play 5 Free Play Games.

Wheel Bonus

  • Press GO to spin the wheel. Advance around the wheel until you hit COLLECT or reach the top prize.

Gift Bonus

  • Select one of three gifts. Instantly win the prize amount shown inside.

Free Play Games

  • The prize multiplier meter resets after each play.
  • FREE PLAY blocks will not appear during Free Play Games.
